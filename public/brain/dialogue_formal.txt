Formal Indonesian Dialogue:

“Selamat pagi, Bapak/Ibu. Terima kasih atas waktu yang telah Anda luangkan untuk pertemuan hari ini.”

“Dengan hormat, saya ingin menyampaikan beberapa poin penting terkait proyek yang sedang berjalan.”

“Saya berharap kerja sama ini dapat memberikan hasil yang optimal bagi kedua belah pihak.”

Formal English Equivalents:
“Good morning, Sir/Madam. Thank you for taking the time to meet with us today.”

“Respectfully, I would like to present several key points regarding the project currently in progress.”

“It is my hope that this collaboration will yield optimal results for both parties.”
